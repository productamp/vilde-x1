## Comparison Model (Three Elements)

The market can be evaluated across three core elements:
1. Website builder
2. AI
3. CMS

## Snapshot Comparison

| Competitor | Website Builder | AI | CMS | Typical Strength |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| WordPress | High | Low-Medium | High | Mature CMS ecosystem and flexibility |
| Wix | High | Medium | Medium | Fast SMB setup with guided builder flow |
| Squarespace | High | Medium | Medium | Polished templates and easy site setup |
| Framer | High | Medium | Low-Medium | Modern visual design and interaction control |
| Webflow | High | Medium | High | Professional visual build + strong CMS workflows |
| Lovable | Medium | High | Low-Medium | AI-first generation speed and iteration |
| v0 | Medium | High | Low | AI-first UI/site generation from prompts |

## ProductVibe Positioning Aim

ProductVibe is designed as the **balanced option across Website builder + AI + CMS**, tuned for:
- Small business needs
- Quick start and fast first value
- Less-skilled web developers who still need strong outputs

The strategic goal is not to dominate only one axis, but to deliver practical quality across all three in one simple workflow.
