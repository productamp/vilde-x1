# Demo — Small Business from Google Maps

## Audience

Local small business owners (restaurants, salons, clinics, local services, etc.) who have a Google Maps listing but no strong website/content presence.

## Demo story

This demo shows one simple, high-impact flow:

1. **Start with Google Maps link**
   - User provides their Google Maps business link.
   - ProductVibe uses that as the starting context.

2. **Generate website directly**
   - Product creates a full marketing website from the business context.
   - Includes practical local-business sections (services, about, contact, location, hours, CTA).

3. **Generate content they can use (blog default)**
   - Product creates usable content items aligned to the business and local audience (blog post by default).
   - Content is editable and ready for ongoing publishing.

## Why this demo works

- Extremely relatable input: every local business already has Maps.
- Shows speed from real-world data point to real website.
- Demonstrates both website creation and growth content in one flow.

## Success criteria

- Google Maps link accepted and used as input context.
- Complete website generated from that input.
- At least one content item (blog post by default) generated and editable.
- Owner understands they can keep publishing content after launch.
